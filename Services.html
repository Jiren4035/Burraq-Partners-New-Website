Deals Advisory

We empower organizations to navigate complex financial landscapes by offering end-to-end Deals Advisory services that drive strategic growth and maximize stakeholder value. Our expertise spans the entire transaction lifecycle, from sell-side and buy-side advisory to rigorous financial and tax due diligence for Mergers & Acquisitions and IPOs. We specialize in business valuations, feasibility studies, and robust financial modeling that provide the clarity needed for informed decision-making. Whether you are seeking capital structure optimization, debt and equity recapitalization, or navigating Initial Public Offerings (IPOs), our team delivers the insights required to close deals with confidence. Furthermore, we support businesses in transition through operational restructuring, distressed debt advisory, and exit readiness strategies, ensuring your organization is positioned for long-term resilience and market leadership.

Business Advisory

We drive operational excellence and organizational resilience through integrated Business Advisory solutions tailored to the unique challenges of the regional market. Our services focus on the three lines of defense, providing comprehensive support in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), including internal audits, enterprise risk management (ERM), and regulatory violation prevention. We specialize in performance improvement, identifying critical gaps and implementing strategies to optimize operations, sales, and customer service. Our team also addresses the technical complexities of modern enterprise through IT Risk & Assurance, ensuring your infrastructure is secure, compliant, and aligned with business objectives. From strategic human resource consulting and talent integration to navigating the nuances of Family Business governance, we deliver the tools and frameworks necessary to enhance efficiency and maintain a sustainable competitive advantage.

Technology Advisory

Burraq Partners’ Technology Advisory services are designed to drive innovation, efficiency, and security for your organization. We combine strategic insights with hands-on expertise to deliver solutions tailored to your business needs.

Tax, Zakat, Legal Services & Process Solutions

We adds value by offering comprehensive zakat and tax planning and compliance services that optimize tax efficiency and ensure full regulatory adherence. We specialize in transaction tax, tax due diligence for merger & acquisition and IPOs, transfer pricing, and international taxation, while also providing expertise in VAT, real estate taxes, and zakat base calculation. Additionally, we support family businesses with zakat & tax prospective, help navigate corporate governance requirements, and offer tailored tax technology solutions. From compliance and bookkeeping to tax dispute resolution and assessment. We deliver strategic insights that minimize risk and enhance business performance at every stage.

Corporate Services & Business Setup Solutions

Corporate Services are foundational to regional success. We empower businesses, especially those entering the Saudi and broader MENA markets, by offering end-to-end guidance on legal establishment, regulatory compliance, and optimal corporate structuring. We navigate the complexities of local commercial laws, licensing requirements, and governance standards to ensure a seamless, efficient, and fully compliant launch. Our expertise covers everything from entity formation and licensing in mainland and free zones to ongoing corporate secretarial duties and regulatory filing. We provide strategic insights that minimize legal risk and lay the groundwork for long-term operational success.

HR Consulting & Secondment

Our Human Resources Consulting and Talent Secondment services are designed to equip your organization with the right talent and the compliant policies necessary for high performance in the MENA region. We recognize that KSA Labor Law and local talent market dynamics present unique challenges. We offer strategic HR policy development, compliance audits, performance management system design, and specialized Talent Secondment. Secondment provides immediate access to expert, project-based staff (from HR specialists to finance controllers) without the commitment of permanent employment, ensuring flexibility and rapid scaling during market expansion or critical projects. We deliver strategic insights that minimize labor risk and ensure your workforce is engaged, compliant, and optimized for success.
