About Burraq

Empowering Growth Through Collaboration Innovation, And Integrity Together, We Shape Success.

Our Story

We are a regional advisory firm with presence across Bahrain, United Kingdom and Canada, with our headquarters in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We partner with family-owned enterprises, SMEs, industry-leading corporations and financial institutions in addressing their challenging business needs. We believe in the spirit of deep collaboration and leverage on a community of highly experienced and seasoned professionals — leading in their respective fields of deals, business advisory, tax and technology.

Our Purpose

At Burraq Partners, we have one simple statement that encapsulates our broader purpose as a regional business consulting firm: "Together We Grow". We try to live our purpose through our work every day by focusing on four purpose principles.

01

We challenge traditional thinking and ways of operating and bring new perspectives to the toughest problems.

02

We discover unique sources of competitive advantage and hidden truths in dynamic, complex systems.

03

We confront the hard issues, staying true to our values, and stating our views candidly and directly.

04

We empower clients, colleagues, and communities to achieve success, growing together by uplifting others.
