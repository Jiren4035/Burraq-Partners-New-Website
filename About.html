About Burraq

Empowering growth through strategic innovation. At Burraq Partners, your vision meets our expertise to deliver tailored, high-impact results.

Our Story

We are an evolving regional advisory firm with a dynamic presence across Bahrain, United Kingdom and Canada, anchored by our headquarters in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, that partners with entities ranging from family-owned enterprises and SMEs to industry-leading corporations and financial enterprises in business and society to tackle their most important challenges and capture their greatest opportunities.

Our success depends on a spirit of deep collaboration and a community of highly experienced and seasoned professionals— gurus in their respective fields of deals, business, tax and technology advisory determined to make the world and each other better every day.
