Our Partners

Strategic alliances with leading organizations to provide comprehensive and specialized solutions for our clients.

Khalij Group

Khalij Group is a conglomerate focused on servicing the needs and requirements of the Islamic financial services industry and Islamic capital markets, including creating innovative and be-spoke structured solutions, fund raising and distribution of both debt and equity instruments. In the United Kingdom, Khalij Group is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.

SHMA Consulting - Actuarial Evaluation

SHMA is a leading advisory firm with offices in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Pakistan, serving clients across MENA and beyond. Our work spans three core areas: Actuarial & Insurance Advisory, Risk Management & Risk Advisory, and End-of-Service Benefits (EOSB). Serving over 450 clients worldwide, we provide actuarial valuations, strategy design, and accounting support. At SHMA, we are committed to simplifying complexity, enabling informed decisions, and creating long-term value for our clients.

AHCPA - External Audit

ABDULRAHMAN KHALED ALHAMAZANI Certified Public Accountants Company provides rigorous external audit capabilities and financial advisory services. Led by experts with deep experience from the Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority and Big Four auditing firms, AHCPA ensures all financial reporting meets statutory requirements and international standards. Their team of specialists contributes to providing high-quality services in auditing, tax, and zakat, fostering institutional trust and transparency.
